How do I start a food search and get food choice reccomendations?

Muncheck is an easy way to find nutritional information and know if a food item is a healthy choice according to your health status.

To start a search and find recommendations:

  • 1. Write a food item and the quantity in the text box of the main screen.
  • 2. Choose an option of your health status or fitness goal by clicking one of the buttons below your food search. You may choose more than one.
  • 3. Press the button below to check the food item and get a recommendation
